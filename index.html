interview with

MEGAN FAVERO

by NUTTA SUKPRASERT

NS—Why do you want to be a designer?

MF—I want to be a designer because I love to express my creativity. I have always been interested in using the computer to create rather than drawing or painting. I think graphic design is interesting because it can be anything such as a poster or a website. I want to be a designer because I can explore many avenues in my field. With my fine arts degree I hope to combine techniques I learned in school and use it to add my own twist onto graphic design.

NS—What worries you about being a designer?

MF—What worries me about being a designer would be finding a job after I finish school. I know there are many job opportunities out there for designers but I think the issue I will face after I graduate will be finding a job I truly enjoy doing everyday.

NS—What excites you about it?

MF—What excites me about design is that it is ever changing. In society today graphic design surrounds us in every way, whether it be in advertising or simple street signs. I think it is exciting to be apart of impacting society in a creative way. It is also exciting to find something that you truly enjoy doing and can see yourself doing for the rest of your life.

NS—What aspect of design do you gravitate towards most?

MF—An aspect of design I gravitate towards the most would be motion graphics. I have always been interested in the idea of moving images or animation. I like the idea of having your designs being able to interact with your audience through movement instead of static images.

NS—What role or responsibility does a designer play in our society?

MF—I think designers play a huge role in our society. Learning about graphic design I have learned that design is all around us, not just in posters. We learned about the New York subway system and that a designer had created all the signs to make navigating the system easier. After learning about this I realized that a designer can shape and change how we look at simple things in life.

NS—What is a website?

MF—I think a website is a page located on the world wide web held under a domain name, it can be for businesses, social media platforms, educational or games. A website is an interaction between the user and the computer.
